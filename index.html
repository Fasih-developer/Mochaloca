Why we drink coffee?

Coffee contains caffeine, a natural stimulant that can help increase alertness and reduce the perception of fatigue. Many people rely on coffee to help them wake up and stay awake, especially in the morning or during long working hours. For many, coffee is more than just a beverage; it's a flavorful experience. Different types of coffee beans and brewing methods can produce a wide range of tastes and aromas, appealing to coffee enthusiasts. Coffee is often enjoyed in social settings. Cafes and coffee shops provide spaces for people to meet, work, or relax together, making coffee consumption a social activity.
